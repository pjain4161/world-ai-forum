About WAIF

The World AI Forum (WAIF) is an international research think tank dedicated to advancing the research, application, and responsible adoption of artificial intelligence. WAIF is joined by members from 10 countries and 5 continents. It connects academia, industry, governments, and civil society to ensure AI innovations contribute to inclusive growth, sustainable development, and societal well-being.

Its activities include flagship global conferences, training programs, publications, and collaborative projects with leading institutions worldwide. Through such initiatives, WAIF emphasizes applied AI, bridging the gap between theoretical advances and real-world solutions. The International Conference on Applied Artificial Intelligence (ICAAI) is WAIF’s flagship event.

Previously WAIF has successfully concluded America-Asia Interaction 2025, International Panel Session on Responsible AI, International Panel Symposium on Innovation and Entrepreneurship 2024, Women in AI – International Symposium, International Start-Up Symposium and Applied AI Flagship Conference. Their events have been joined by distinguished speakers from Google, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft including CEOs, Founders and Venture Capital Firms etc.
